New to OneKey Perps?

Get started in minutes

No account, no KYC, no switching apps. Everything lives inside the OneKey App.

1

Download the OneKey App

Available on iOS and Android. The app is your self-custodied wallet — you control your keys at all times. Download here →

2

Create or import your wallet

Set up a new wallet in seconds, or import an existing one with your seed phrase. No email, no password, no KYC required.

3

Open the Perps tab

Tap Trade → Perps inside the app, or visit app.onekey.so/perps. You'll see 260+ tradable assets — crypto, forex, commodities, equities, and more.

4

Deposit USDC and start trading

Deposit USDC to your Perps balance. Choose an asset, set your leverage (up to 50x), pick long or short, and confirm. Your volume starts counting toward the competition immediately.

5

Enter via your KOL's invite link

Make sure you join the competition through your KOL's exclusive invite link — this links your trading activity to the event leaderboard.
